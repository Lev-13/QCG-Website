  • Open Projects 2023

    Open projects were released for all students of IIT Roorkee, where any student from the institute could participate and complete the given project over a period of 4 weeks.

  • Workshop spring 2023

    QCG conducted a discussion series for all students of IIT Roorkee, but primarily focused towards the freshers batch.

  • Dead and Alive 2022

    QCG conducted “Dead & Alive”, the annual Qiskit Fall Fest, in collaboration with IBM Qiskit, from 4th to 6th November 2022. The hackathon served to provide a platform for students to learn and explore the vast and intriguing domain of quantum computing through innovative projects and prompts.

  • PaAC X QCG Student Talk Series

    The Quantum Computing Group (QCG), in collaboration with the Physics and Astronomy Club (PaAC), conducted a series of student seminars covering a wide range of topics in the field of Many Body Quantum Theory and its implications in the domain of Quantum Computation and Information.

  • Quantum Computing using Amazon Braket

    The workshop organised by QCG in association with AWS in the last week of August'22 was a huge success, receiving participation in large numbers from the IITR Junta.

  • IITR Quantum Hackathon 2021

    IITR Quantum Hackathon'21, organised by QCG IIT Roorkee and sponsored by IBM Quantum as a part of Qiskit Fall Fest, brought students with different backgrounds together from 16th-19th October 2021 to team up and build quantum computing projects using Qiskit.
